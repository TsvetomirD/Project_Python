F1 MCL60 SHOWCASE

Introduction

What is MCL60?

The McLaren MCL60 is a Formula One car designed and constructed by McLaren, initially under the direction of James Key, to compete in the 2023 Formula One World Championship.

Debut

The car made its competitive début at the 2023 Bahrain Grand Prix. It was driven by Lando Norris and Oscar Piastri, Norris for his fifth season with McLaren and Piastri in his rookie year.

Achievements

British Grand Prix (Silverstone) - Lando Norris finished in 2nd place, and Oscar Piastri came in 4th. Hungarian Grand Prix - Norris secured another 2nd place finish, while Piastri finished 5th. Japanese Grand Prix - Both drivers achieved podium finishes, with Norris in 2nd place and Piastri in 3rd. Qatar Grand Prix - Piastri won the Sprint race and finished 2nd in the main race, while Norris finished 3rd.
